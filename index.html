About Us




We are highly professional Transport Management Training and Consultancy firm, based out of Kilkenny City.



We are currently performing consultations through various formats - our preferred offering would be a face to face meeting, however due to the current Covid-19 travel restrictions we are facilitating remote online sessions via virtual classrooms on the Zoom platform. To find out more, hit the button below.

Established in Kilkenny in 2006, we have been providing a full range of professional transport management and training services nationwide ever since. Over that period we have built and maintained strong relationships with our clients and pride ourselves on the quality of service we provide. We are always delighted to meet new potential clients and would be happy to discuss your business needs.
